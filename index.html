Smart by design

Jailbreaker runs tasks in the background periodically to check for certain things like game moderators joining your server, sounds playing too loud and other users spamming the chat system.

When these background checks are triggered, they will trigger Jailbreaker to act upon them, for example, Jailbreaker will suppress loud audios if it's detected that the audio playing is too loud.

Sound Suppressed

A high volume audio is being played (Music) and it has been suppressed

Spam Detection

We've automatically muted @Jack for spamming the chat system, they will be unmuted in 90 seconds

Moderator Detection

We've identified a moderator in the server you're in, see the prompt at the top of your screen to take action

Peace of mind.

Jailbreaker also offers several features to improve your experience. It can prevent interruptions due to inactivity and stop client-based kicks from occuring. It also automatically adjusts FPS when you switch between programs and notifies you of high latency or low FPS, while also taking automatic action to combat the low frame times. These features can enhance your overall experience and make it smoother and more enjoyable.
